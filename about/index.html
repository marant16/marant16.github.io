About me #

  • I’m a guy in my early 20s from Lima, Perú.

  • Since I was little, I’ve been drawn to science and engineering.

  • The public school system here is not very demanding, so after finishing high school I felt lost and began to self-study math, coding, and physics (shoutout to these wonderful people).

  • I learned to speak English by watching YouTube videos and scrolling through Reddit.

  • I studied 3 semesters of electrical engineering at a local private university, but I dropped out due to financial difficulties and general disappointment with the state of education.

  • For most of my life I struggled with unhealthy technology use and a lack of purpose, until I discovered this guy who helped me understand how I work.

About this website #

This website mainly serves as:

  • A place to record and organize my thoughts and ideas.

  • A way to let anyone interested know who I am, what I do and how I think.

  • A way to share what I’ve learned with anybody who could benefit from it.
